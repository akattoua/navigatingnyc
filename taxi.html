when in doubt... "Taxi!"

Hailing an New York City Taxi can be one of the easiest activities or one of the most taxing, but it doesn't have to be. (And yes, that pun was intended).

By following a few simple steps, hailing a taxi can, usually go smoothly and with little cramping of shoulders as possible.

  1. Find a corner. You'll have far better luck
  2. Wait for cab with the roof number light on
  3. Stand on an avenue that is more heavily trafficked
  4. Pro-tip: find a street or avenue where the traffic is going the direction you are going. This will save you both time and money.
