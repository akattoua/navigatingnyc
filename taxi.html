When all else fails..."TAXI!"

Hailing an New York City Taxi can be one of the easiest activities or one of the moset taxing, but it doesnt have to be. (And yes, that pun was intended).

By following a few simple steps, hailing a taxi can, usually go smoothly and with little cramping of sholders as possible.

  1. Find a corner. You'll have far better luck
  2. Wait for the roof light
  3. Stand on an avenue that is more heavily trafficked
  4. Pro-tip: find a street or avenue where the traffic is going the direction you are going. This will save you both time and money.
